Make 9

Make 9 is an incredible brain challenging game. It starts off easy buy takes efforts to be an expert. And it’ll be a perfect choice if you love number games, block games, or other similar brain teasing games.  The game is easy to start. Simply move identical numbers together and they will be merged into a bigger number. Your ultimate mission is to merge and make ‘9’,‘19’,‘29’... all the way to the final goal–‘99’.  Making the first ‘9’ might be easy but it will be a huge challenge to get to the final ‘99’. Try it now and see if you could be the first player to work out ‘99’!
